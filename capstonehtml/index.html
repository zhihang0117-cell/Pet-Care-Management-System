FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about PAwfect AI’s use, setup, booking flow, AI support, and staff control.

What is PAwfect AI?

PAwfect AI is an AI-powered pet care management system for grooming, boarding, and daycare businesses. It helps manage WhatsApp enquiries, bookings, pet profiles, daily updates, staff tasks, and analytics.

Does PAwfect AI only support WhatsApp?

The main customer communication channel is WhatsApp. Customers send enquiries through WhatsApp, while staff manage the structured results through the web-based dashboard.

Can PAwfect AI automatically confirm bookings?

PAwfect AI can prepare booking suggestions based on service availability and business rules. Final confirmation can still require staff approval through the human-in-the-loop review process.

Can the system manage grooming, boarding, and daycare?

Yes. The platform supports grooming appointment records, boarding room or occupancy control, and daycare attendance or daily care tracking.

Why must businesses complete configuration first?

Configuration is needed because dashboards depend on business hours, service packages, grooming settings, room capacity, daycare capacity, policy documents, and operating rules.

Does PAwfect AI replace staff?

No. PAwfect AI reduces repetitive manual work, but staff still handle pet care, special cases, final approvals, and customer relationship decisions.
