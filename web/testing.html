Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about setting up PAwfect AI in your business.

What is PAwfect AI exactly?

PAwfect AI is an all-in-one, premium pet care operations system built for boutique grooming, boarding, and daycare businesses. It safely connects your conversational platforms like WhatsApp with backend business configurations to streamline scheduling and reduce administrative errors.

Does PAwfect AI require replacing our current contact channels?

No. Your customers continue messaging your business using their standard WhatsApp app. The platform acts behind the scenes, parsing those unstructured requests and populating them cleanly inside your manager workspace.

Can the AI automatically confirm bookings or change prices?

No. PAwfect AI operates under strict Human-in-the-Loop workflows. The system reviews parameters, scans availability limits, and prepares suggestions, but your staff always gives the final confirmation before clients are updated.

Why must businesses configure their services first?

To keep responses accurate, the system needs to know your custom rules. Entering specific capacity levels, operating hours, package rates, and kennel availability ensures the AI works within your actual operational boundaries.
